Our Mission

Winnersville Community Center exists to help people find God by creating environments that improve the quality of life in individuals by developing a healthy MIND, BODY, and SPIRIT!

Josh Sullivan Beat the Streets

Josh Sullivan Beat the Streets Program is a program to increase the graduation rate for at least 10 At-Risk Middle/High School students. This will include an annual event in November each year to raise funds and awareness for the Josh Sullivan Beat the Streets Program at the Winnersville Community Center. The program consists of a one hour CrossFit and one hour of tutoring/ completing homework for students 6th - 12th grade Monday through Thursday with a devotional instead of tutoring on Friday, as well as trips, camps and more.

Community Groups

Winnersville Community Center Community Groups are designed to build up believers yet still attract unbelievers. A place were you can connect with God and with other people on a regular basis. Join one today by just showing up or contact Polly Barcol for more details.
WOMEN'S COMMUNITY GROUPS @ WCC
Join us the 2nd Monday of every month at 9:30am at Winnersville Community Center, 2037 E Park Ave, for fun and time in God's Word.
COUPLE’S COMMUNITY GROUPS @ WCC
Join us the 2nd Thursday of every month at 6:00pm at Winnersville Community Center, 2037 E Park Ave, for fun with other couples and time in God's Word.
WEIGHT OF GRACE COMMUNITY GROUP @ WCC
Join us the 1st Thursday of the month at 6:00pm at Winnersville Community Center to find freedom from overeating and develop a healthier relationship with God and food.

Fitness & Faith Workouts

Are you ready for a different kind of Sunday that includes a work out with a spiritual kick? Join WCC and CFW family at Winnersville Community Center on the 3RD SUNDAY of the month for a Fitness & Faith WOD. We will have a typical CrossFit training session and a non-CrossFit workout, as well as workouts for teens and children, from 10:00am - 11:00am. Then, from 11:00am - 11:30am, we'll grab a FitAid and a foam roller while we hear a powerful message from communicators either live or via video. This WOD is open and free to the community, so invite all your friends, family, neighbors, and co-workers who don't go to church!

Community Service

At Winnersville Community Center, it is essential that we express God’s love out to the community. Here are a few projects we do annually and organizations we partner with to improve the quality of life in our community:
THE PINES FAMILY CAMPUS -
Thanksgiving Meal Baskets: We provide a full thanksgiving dinner for all families who reside at the Pines Family Campus, a one year rehabilitation facility.
Christmas Gifts:
We provide a $25 gift card for all the Moms at the Pines. We will also provide a pair of shoes, a shirt, a pair of pants, and one toy for every child at the Pines.
Easter Celebration:
We provide a Free Easter Egg Hunt for all the children at the Pines as well as lunch for the whole family!
J.L. NEWBERN -
Back to School Back Packs: We provide new back packs for a minimum of 250 students at JL Newborn at the beginning of each school year.

CrossFit Winnersville

CrossFit Winnersville is a WCC partner in ministry. CrossFit Winnersville is a CrossFit affiliate that meets inside WCC. Our goal is to be physically prepared for all the challenges that life may throw at us. We will increase your athletic ability by covering all aspects of fitness.

Life Ministires

Life Ministries Valdosta is a WCC partner in ministry. Our mission is to equip believers to live life to the fullest (John 10:10).

Life Coaching

A catalyst for personal growth. Life Coaching is a monthly one on one relationship with your coach who helps you discover your potential.

Life Mentoring

Finding freedom and joy with Christ as our life. (Galatians 2:20). Life Mentoring is a weekly one on one relationship with your mentor who leads you in freeing truths about who you are and who God is.

Life Training

Training in the Exchanged Life to become a Life Mentor is a year long training with class meeting weekly led by Polly Barcol.
